Methodology and Sources

Sources

Data Processing Steps

  • Transcription & Digitization: Converting archival handwritten and typewritten records into a structured Excel format.
  • Unification & Normalization: Standardizing names and surnames to resolve inconsistencies found in the original documents.
  • Toponymic Correction: Identifying and correcting historical place names and mapping them to modern locations.
  • Cross-Verification: Aligning the registry data with L. A. Gross‚Äôs diaries to clarify the journey's timeline.
  • Cleaning & Validation: Removing duplicates, verifying birth years, and finalizing the working data structure.
  • Technical Transformation: Exporting the cleaned data to JSON format for instant search functionality among 2,403 records.
